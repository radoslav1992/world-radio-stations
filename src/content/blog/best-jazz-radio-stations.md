Jazz is one of the genres radio does best. A good jazz station is more than a playlist — it's curation, mood and a sense of time of day. Here's how to find great jazz online, wherever you are. The quickest route is our [Jazz & Soul collection](/moods/jazz), which pulls jazz stations from around the world into one place.

## Public radio's jazz heritage

In the United States, public stations have long been guardians of jazz. [KCRW](/stations/kcrw) and other public outlets weave jazz into eclectic, expertly curated programming, while many NPR member stations run dedicated jazz shows. Their music staff bring real depth and context to the music.

**Best for:** curated jazz with knowledgeable hosts.

## Dedicated jazz streams

Beyond the big names, there are hundreds of specialist jazz stations online — smooth jazz, bebop, vocal jazz, fusion and soul-jazz, broadcasting from cities all over the world. Many run with little or no talk, making them ideal background listening.

**Best for:** uninterrupted jazz for work, dinner or a slow evening.
