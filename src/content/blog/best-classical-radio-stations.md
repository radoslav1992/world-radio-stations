Classical music and radio are a natural pair — there's nothing like a well-chosen symphony filling a room. Online, the choice is enormous, from accessible popular-classical stations to deep, serious catalogues. Here's where to start, all streamable [for free](/).

## Accessible classical

[Classic FM](/stations/classic-fm-uk) in the UK pioneered the popular-classical format: well-known works, film scores and relaxed presentation that welcomes newcomers. It's the perfect entry point if "classical radio" sounds intimidating.

**Best for:** easing into classical music without the formality.

## Public broadcasters' classical services

Public broadcasters across Europe and beyond run dedicated classical channels with serious depth — full concerts, opera, contemporary composition and expert presentation. Use the [player](/#player) to explore: pick a country like Germany, France or the UK and look for their classical services, or tap the **Classical** genre filter.

**Best for:** deep listening, full works and live concert recordings.

## Calm, focused listening

Classical music is also one of the best soundtracks for concentration. If you're studying or working, our [Focus collection](/moods/focus) gathers calm, low-distraction stations — many of them instrumental and classical-leaning — into one place.

**Best for:** studying, working and winding down.

Open the [player](/#player), choose a country and use the **Classical** filter, or jump straight into a curated [mood collection](/moods). Save your favourites and they'll be ready whenever you need a quiet moment.
