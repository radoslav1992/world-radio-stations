Canada's radio landscape stretches across six time zones and two official languages, from big-city commercial hits to remote community broadcasters. Here are some of the best Canadian stations you can [stream online for free](/) — and you can browse the full list by choosing **Canada** in the [player](/#player).

## CBC Radio One — the national conversation

[CBC Radio One](/stations/cbc-radio-one) is the cornerstone of Canadian public radio. Part of the Canadian Broadcasting Corporation, it combines national news, current affairs and documentaries with strong local programming in each region. If you want to understand what's happening across Canada, start here.

**Best for:** news, current affairs and documentary-style shows.

## Commercial pop and rock

Canada's commercial stations cover every mainstream taste — contemporary hits, classic rock, country and adult contemporary. Major markets like Toronto, Vancouver and Montréal have especially crowded dials, and most of these stations stream online. Use the search box in the player to find a specific brand, or scroll the Canada list to discover something new.

**Best for:** chart hits, classic rock and everyday listening.

## French-language radio

Canada's French-language stations — concentrated in Québec and francophone communities elsewhere — are a world of their own, with their own stars, news services and music scene. They're a great resource if you're learning French or want a different musical perspective.

**Best for:** French listeners and language learners.
