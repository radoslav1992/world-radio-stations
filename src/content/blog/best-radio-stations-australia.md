Australia punches well above its weight in radio, with a strong public broadcaster and a lively commercial scene. Whether you want new music, news or talkback, here are some of the best Australian stations to [stream online](/) — browse the full list by selecting **Australia** in the [player](/#player).

## triple j — new and alternative music

[triple j](/stations/triple-j) is the ABC's youth music station and one of the most influential outlets for new music anywhere. It champions Australian artists, runs the famous Hottest 100 countdown, and is the place to hear tomorrow's big names first.

**Best for:** alternative, indie and new Australian music.

## ABC news and local radio

The Australian Broadcasting Corporation runs a network of news and local stations covering the whole country, including remote and regional areas. They're a reliable source of news, weather and emergency information as well as local culture.

**Best for:** news, current affairs and local coverage.

## Commercial hits

Australia's commercial networks dominate the city dials with contemporary hits, classic rock and talkback. Most stream online, so you can tune into Sydney, Melbourne or Brisbane from anywhere in the world.

**Best for:** chart music and big-personality breakfast shows.
