## History

CBC Radio One is the English-language news and information service of the Canadian Broadcasting Corporation, the national public broadcaster founded in 1936. It operates as a network of local stations across the country, each combining national programming with regional news.

## Format and programming

The station is built around news, current affairs, documentaries and cultural programming, with little or no commercial advertising. Local morning and afternoon shows bookend nationally distributed programs, giving listeners both regional coverage and a shared national conversation.

## Reach

CBC Radio One reaches the whole of Canada through a network of local transmitters and streams online via CBC Listen, serving urban and remote communities alike.

## Significance

As Canada's principal public talk-radio service, CBC Radio One is a central institution in Canadian media, valued for its journalism and its role in connecting a vast, multi-time-zone country.
