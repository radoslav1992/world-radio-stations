## History

Radio Deejay launched in 1982 and became one of Italy's most influential commercial music stations, helping shape the country's contemporary radio style. It is part of the GEDI media group.

## Format and programming

The station combines a pop and dance playlist with strong personality-led programming, comedy and entertainment formats. Its presenters have become well-known figures in Italian popular culture, and the station has long balanced music with humour and live shows.

## Reach

Radio Deejay broadcasts nationally across Italy on FM and DAB+ digital radio and streams online, reaching audiences throughout the country and abroad.

## Significance

Radio Deejay is a landmark of Italian commercial radio, recognised for its mix of current music and entertainment and its impact on the national radio landscape.
