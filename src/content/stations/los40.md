## History

LOS40 — long known as Los 40 Principales — launched in 1966 as a chart-based music station and grew into one of the most recognisable music radio brands in the Spanish-speaking world. It is operated by PRISA Radio.

## Format and programming

The station plays a contemporary hit format of current Spanish and international pop, supported by chart countdowns, presenter shows and high-profile live music events and awards. It maintains a strong connection with a younger audience across radio and digital platforms.

## Reach

LOS40 broadcasts on an extensive FM network across Spain and streams online, with related LOS40 stations operating in several Spanish-speaking countries.

## Significance

LOS40 is a defining pop radio brand in Spain and Latin America, closely associated with the contemporary music scene in the Spanish language.
