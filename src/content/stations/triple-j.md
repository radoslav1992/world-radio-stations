## History

triple j began in 1975 as 2JJ, a Sydney youth station run by the Australian Broadcasting Corporation, and later expanded into a national network. It has built a strong identity around alternative music and support for the local scene.

## Format and programming

The station focuses on new and alternative music with a heavy emphasis on Australian artists, supported by presenter shows, live sessions and features like Like A Version. Its annual Hottest 100 — a listener-voted countdown of the year's favourite songs — has become a major cultural event in Australia.

## Reach

triple j broadcasts nationally on FM and DAB+ and streams worldwide via the ABC listen platform.

## Significance

triple j is one of the most influential outlets for new music in Australia, regularly launching the careers of local artists and shaping the national music conversation.
