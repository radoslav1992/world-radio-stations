## History

NPO Radio 2 is part of the Netherlands' public broadcasting system, with roots in the long history of Dutch public radio. In its modern form it serves as the country's main pop and rock station for adult listeners.

## Format and programming

The station plays a broad mix of pop and rock spanning recent decades, with presenter-led shows and a focus on familiar, melodic music. It is especially well known for the *Top 2000*, an annual listener-voted countdown of favourite songs broadcast around the end of the year, which has become a national tradition.

## Reach

NPO Radio 2 broadcasts nationally across the Netherlands on FM and DAB+ and streams online via the NPO platform.

## Significance

NPO Radio 2 is one of the most popular public radio stations in the Netherlands, and its Top 2000 has become one of the country's best-known radio events.
