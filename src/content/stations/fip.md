## History

FIP began in 1971 as part of Radio France and developed a singular identity built around music rather than presenters. For decades it has been admired for a programming philosophy that puts the listening experience first.

## Format and programming

FIP is known for its eclectic selection — jazz, world music, classical, rock, electronic, chanson and more — woven together with very little talk and no commercials. The station's hushed, carefully curated style has earned it a devoted following well beyond France.

## Reach

FIP broadcasts on FM in Paris and a number of other French cities, on DAB+ digital radio nationally, and streams online with several themed sub-stations through Radio France.

## Significance

FIP is often cited internationally as a model of public music radio, valued for its curation and its commitment to discovery across genres.
