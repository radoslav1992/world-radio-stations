## History

RTÉ Radio 1 traces its origins to 1926 and the launch of Irish national radio, originally known by the call sign 2RN. As the senior station of public broadcaster RTÉ, it has been a central part of Irish broadcasting for almost a century.

## Format and programming

The station is built around news, current affairs and talk, complemented by documentaries, drama, arts and music programming. Its flagship daytime news and discussion shows are among the most influential in Ireland.

## Reach

RTÉ Radio 1 broadcasts nationally across Ireland on FM and DAB and streams worldwide via the RTÉ platform, reaching the Irish public and the diaspora abroad.

## Significance

As Ireland's principal public radio station, RTÉ Radio 1 holds a central place in the country's media landscape and public life.
