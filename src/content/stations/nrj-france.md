## History

NRJ launched in Paris in 1981, during the opening up of France's FM airwaves to private stations. It grew rapidly into one of the country's most popular music networks and the cornerstone of the NRJ Group, which later expanded the brand to several other countries.

## Format and programming

NRJ focuses on contemporary hit radio: current pop, dance and urban hits aimed at a young audience. Its schedule combines a fast-moving playlist with popular presenter shows, events and music awards.

## Reach

NRJ broadcasts nationally across France on FM and DAB+ and streams online, with related NRJ-branded stations operating internationally.

## Significance

NRJ is one of the defining commercial pop radio brands in France and continental Europe, and a major force in the French music market.
