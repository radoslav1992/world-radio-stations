## History

1LIVE launched in 1995 as a youth radio service of Westdeutscher Rundfunk (WDR), the public broadcaster for the German state of North Rhine-Westphalia. It was created to appeal to younger listeners with a modern, music-led format.

## Format and programming

The station plays current pop and chart music, complemented by presenter-driven shows, comedy, listener interaction and coverage of music and youth culture. It has become one of the most listened-to youth stations in Germany.

## Reach

1LIVE broadcasts across North Rhine-Westphalia on FM and DAB+ and streams online via WDR, making it accessible nationwide and internationally over the internet.

## Significance

1LIVE is one of Germany's leading young-audience radio brands and a notable example of public broadcasting tailored to a youth market.
