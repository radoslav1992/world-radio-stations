## History

Z100, with the call sign WHTZ, launched its contemporary hit format in 1983 and quickly rose to the top of the competitive New York radio market. Its fast climb in the ratings became a widely cited case study in radio programming, and the station has remained a fixture of the city's pop landscape ever since.

It is owned by iHeartMedia, one of the largest radio companies in the United States.

## Format and programming

Z100 plays current Top 40 hits, with a schedule built around high-profile morning and afternoon shows, listener interaction and frequent artist appearances. The station is also known for large-scale events such as its annual holiday concert.

## Reach

The station broadcasts on 100.3 FM in the New York metropolitan area and streams nationally and internationally through the iHeartRadio platform.

## Significance

As the flagship CHR station in the largest U.S. market, Z100 is one of the most influential pop radio brands in the country and a frequent launch pad for hit singles.
