## History

RMF FM launched in 1990, in the early years of commercial radio in post-communist Poland, and grew into one of the country's biggest and most popular stations. It is the flagship of Grupa RMF.

## Format and programming

The station plays a mainstream mix of Polish and international pop hits, paired with regular news bulletins, traffic information and entertainment programming. Its broad, accessible format has kept it among Poland's most-listened-to stations for years.

## Reach

RMF FM broadcasts through an extensive FM network across Poland and streams online, with a family of themed sub-stations available digitally.

## Significance

RMF FM is one of the defining commercial radio brands in Poland and a consistent leader in national listenership.
