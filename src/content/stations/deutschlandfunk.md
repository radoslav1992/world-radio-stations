## History

Deutschlandfunk was established in 1962 and today forms part of Deutschlandradio, the national public radio body that produces nationwide, advertising-free programming for Germany. It has a long tradition as a serious news and information service.

## Format and programming

The station concentrates on journalism: regular news bulletins, political and economic analysis, science and culture features and extended current-affairs discussion. Music plays a smaller role than on many stations, with the emphasis firmly on spoken-word content.

## Reach

Deutschlandfunk broadcasts nationally across Germany on FM and DAB+ digital radio and streams online, reaching listeners throughout the country and abroad.

## Significance

As one of Germany's principal national news radio stations, Deutschlandfunk is a key reference for in-depth journalism and public-affairs coverage in the German language.
