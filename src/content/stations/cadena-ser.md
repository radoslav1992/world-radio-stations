## History

Cadena SER's origins date back to 1924, making it one of the oldest radio brands in Spain. It developed into a nationwide network and is operated by PRISA Radio, part of one of Spain's major media groups.

## Format and programming

The station is built around news, current affairs, sport and talk, with high-profile morning and late-night programs and extensive live sports coverage. Its mix of journalism, analysis and entertainment talk has made it a national institution.

## Reach

Cadena SER broadcasts through an extensive FM network across Spain and streams online, reaching listeners nationwide and among Spanish speakers abroad.

## Significance

Cadena SER is consistently among the most listened-to radio stations in Spain and a leading voice in Spanish-language news and talk radio.
