## History

P3 is one of the main channels of Sveriges Radio, Sweden's public service broadcaster. It developed into the broadcaster's youth-oriented channel, blending music with spoken-word programming aimed at a younger audience.

## Format and programming

The station mixes contemporary pop and rock with talk, comedy, documentaries and culture. Alongside music shows it is known for popular spoken-word formats and a focus on Swedish artists and stories.

## Reach

P3 broadcasts nationally across Sweden on FM and DAB+ and streams online through Sveriges Radio Play.

## Significance

As Sveriges Radio's flagship young-audience channel, P3 is a prominent voice in Swedish public broadcasting and the country's contemporary music and culture scene.
