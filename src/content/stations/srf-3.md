## History

SRF 3 is part of Schweizer Radio und Fernsehen, the German-language arm of Switzerland's public broadcaster SRG SSR. Launched in the 1980s, it became one of the most popular stations in German-speaking Switzerland.

## Format and programming

The station plays contemporary pop and rock for a broad adult audience, combined with news updates, entertainment segments and a focus on music discovery. It balances a mainstream playlist with coverage of the Swiss music scene.

## Reach

SRF 3 broadcasts across German-speaking Switzerland on FM and DAB+ and streams online via SRF.

## Significance

SRF 3 is one of Switzerland's leading public music stations and a key part of the country's German-language broadcasting landscape.
