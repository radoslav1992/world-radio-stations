## History

France Inter is the flagship generalist network of Radio France, the French public broadcaster. Its roots reach back to the post-war reorganisation of French radio in the late 1940s, and it has since become one of the most listened-to stations in the country.

## Format and programming

The station offers a broad mix of news, in-depth interviews, cultural and satirical programs, debate and music. Its morning news and talk programming is among the most influential in France, and it is known for combining serious journalism with humour and the arts.

## Reach

France Inter broadcasts nationally across France on FM and DAB+ digital radio and streams worldwide through the Radio France platform.

## Significance

As Radio France's principal generalist station, France Inter is a central voice in French media and culture and consistently ranks among the country's most popular radio stations.
