## History

KCRW traces its roots to 1945 and is licensed to Santa Monica College. It developed from a small educational outlet into one of the most prominent public radio stations on the United States' West Coast, blending public-service journalism with a distinctive music identity.

## Format and programming

The station is widely recognised for *Morning Becomes Eclectic*, a long-running music program that mixes new releases across genres with live performances, and for the influence of its music staff on film, television and advertising soundtracks. Alongside music, KCRW carries NPR newsmagazines and produces its own news, culture and food programming.

## Reach

KCRW broadcasts on 89.9 FM across the greater Los Angeles area and streams globally through its website and apps, offering separate music and news streams online.

## Significance

KCRW is considered a leading tastemaker in American music radio, with a reputation for breaking new artists and shaping the sound of Southern California's creative industries.
