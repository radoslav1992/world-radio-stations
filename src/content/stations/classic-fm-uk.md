## History

Classic FM launched in 1992 as the United Kingdom's first national commercial radio station, and the first to bring classical music to a mass commercial audience. It is operated by the media company Global.

## Format and programming

The station's approach is deliberately accessible, mixing well-known classical works, film scores and contemporary classical pieces with relaxed presentation. Schedules are designed around listeners' daily routines, and the station runs popular features such as listener-voted countdowns of favourite works.

## Reach

Classic FM broadcasts nationally across the United Kingdom on FM and DAB digital radio, and streams online through the Global Player platform.

## Significance

Classic FM is widely credited with broadening the audience for classical music in the UK and remains the country's leading classical station by reach.
